Introduction

Tukuntawa Farms is a Nigerian agribusiness venture focused on the production and trading of arable crops

The business was established to take advantage of the significant economic potential of the agricultural sector in Nigeria, which contributes an average of 24% to the country's GDP. The venture aims to leverage innovation technologies and sustainable practices to increase productivity, reduce waste, and minimize the environmental impact of its operations.

Our Farm's operations are guided by the principles of sustainability, efficiency, and ethical practices. We are looking forward to incorporate inovative technoloies such as greenhouses, vertical farmng, and renewable energy sources to optimize resources usage and reduce waste. While leveraging the cuuting-edge technologies such as artificial
intelligence, blockchain, and the internet of things (IOT) to optimize and ensure transparency and traceability throughout the supply chain.

Vision And Mission

Vision:

Becoming a leading agribusiness in Nigeria and globally recognized for its commitment to sustainability,innovation, and social responsibility

Mission

Providing high-quality products to local and international customers while supporting the local economy and minimizing its environmental impact.
